Account Deletion Process

Follow the steps below to request deletion of your Ticket Daddy account and associated data.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Ensure you're signed in to your Ticket Daddy account
  2. Review the data deletion information below
  3. Check the confirmation box
  4. Submit your deletion request

Data Handling Information:

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Personal profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Payment information
  • Event browsing history
  • Saved events and favorites

Data That Will Be Retained:

  • Past transaction records (retained for 7 years for legal compliance)
  • Support ticket history (retained for 2 years)
  • Event attendance records (retained for 1 year)

Note: Account deletion will be processed within 30 days of request submission. Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes as noted above.
